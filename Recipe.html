Jollof Rice

Jollof rice is one of the most loved dishes in West Africa. It is flavorful, colorful, and best enjoyed with family and friends. Many people argue about who makes it best, but at the end, it always tastes good.

Recipe Information

Preparation time: 20 minutes

Cooking time: 45 minutes

Servings: 6 people

Difficulty: Intermediate

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of rice
  • 5 fresh tomatoes
  • 3 red bell peppers
  • 2 medium onions
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 seasoning cubes
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder

Instructions

  1. Blend tomatoes, peppers, and one onion until smooth.
  2. Heat oil in a pot and fry chopped onions for 2 minutes.
  3. Pour in the blended mixture and allow it to cook down.
  4. Add seasoning, curry, and salt. Stir well.
  5. Wash rice and pour into the sauce, then add water.
  6. Cover and cook on medium heat until rice is soft and well cooked.

Tips

For a smoky flavor, allow the rice to burn slightly at the bottom. You can also serve with fried plantain or grilled chicken.
